### My story

I'm originally from Lahore, Pakistan and always being intrigued by computers, internet and tech overall. Also, I always liked to solve riddles and poke around toys to see how they actually works rather than just playing with them (what's fun in that, i know right).

My first exposure to hacking was around 15 through phishing, that I did to my friends to prank them while we were on holidays after finishing school (not my proudest moment). This always stayed with me, and I continued with me life like any averge Aslam, studying and going to engineering school (yayyy!). Little did I know, that I had no interest in it, and I was just following it just as a norm. 

Like often good things happens when you least expect them, I studied some subjects related to computer networks and network security during last year of my university, which got my attention. And that's how my infosec journey started!
